Me and the boys has reached its end of life

It is with great sadness that we regret to inform you that we are ceasing our activities on 30th of May 2023, for an undetermined time. We still do not know if we will ever come back, we simply do not have the time/money due to our life being so busy and the lack of player activity all come to contribute to our decision agreed by all the owners to pull the plug on this server.

If we will ever return, we will try a somewhat different approach to how we manage our server and release content on it.

This was a great adventure for us, and thanks to you all that played and believed in us.

A great farewell from
